Oxford Shoes

Oxford shoe is a formal shoe which are classy, elegant dress shoes available in an array of styles to suit any occasion. They sit under your ankle, have a low heel and, most importantly, the lacing system is stitched closed at the bottom. An open-air trip deep into the heart of nature, updating the outerwear wardrobe in a feminine and colorful array. Suitable for a modern mountain bohemian with looks overflowing in flowers, checks and knits, all layered and blended in with a functional utility style protocol.

Design Story

During the 5th semester, we learnt about the making oxford shoes, and I chose to add woollen checked fabric through forecasting and I have incorporated this in both the footwear and garment.
