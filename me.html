Hi, this is to take you through a piece of mine and a tinge of my creative world.


I firmly believe that the sole purpose of designing is to solve a problem and also make the solution look aesthetically pleasing as well. So all of my designs are made considering these as the primary set of rules. Adding up to this, through the interactions with the artisans and other experienced designers I have come to know that every design has a story behind it and it has kind of rubbed off on me as well.


Also regarding the design themes and aesthetics, as I majorly work on leather and every hide of leather is unique I had developed to keep the realness as well as the rawness of it intact in my designs through the colours, designs and finishes.
