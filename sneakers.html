Sneakers

The Retro quaint is all about self love. This gives out colorful and joyful vibes. This theme also makes us remember the 70's style of fashion like popping colours. Optical wallpaper prints and optimist florals mix in with 70s and 90s volumes . This is inspired by art , nature and retro styling . House parties and late-night celebrations are back on the agenda. This theme conveys to be free , cool and enjoy the present .

Design Story

During the 8 weeks internship period, I designed and made this footwear to apply the learnings practically, in the industry. As it was the first time my product is getting made in the factory, I had made the design and patterns by myself. After analysing the leather types and colours, the suitable leather was chosen based on the learning in the industry as well as college.
