Among Us

"Among Us" is a widely popular multiplayer game that has captured the attention of players worldwide. The game's theme revolves around a group of crewmates working together to complete tasks on a spaceship, all while impostors attempt to sabotage their efforts. The main features are the minimalist and abstract approach to the graphics. The logos and the colours will be major elements to work with.
