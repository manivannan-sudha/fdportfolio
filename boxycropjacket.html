Boxy Crop Jacket

The jacket is the crop, boxy open jacket with full sleeve and notch collar. An open-air trip deep into the heart of nature, updating the outerwear wardrobe in a feminine and colorful array. Suitable for a modern mountain bohemian with looks overflowing in flowers, checks and knits, all layered and blended in with a functional utility style protocol.

Design Story

During the 5th Semester, we were told to make a jacket for women, I chose to do a notch collared crop jacket because cropped tops were also on trend and added the additional design element of woollen checked print in the sleeve and yoke part of the jacket.
