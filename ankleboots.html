Ankle Boots

Ankle boots is a boot that covers the whole foot and cuts off at the ankle or a little above. This boot is made for men using cow leather, interlinings, lining leathers, foam, sock bed, insole, sole, eyelets and shoe lace as major components.A wardrobe of basics most especially made up of raw colours and finishes undergoes renaissance via decorative detailing and fabrics at play: combinations of materials. A chic vibe, simple but timelessly elegant to be worn day-to-day.

Design Story

During the 8 weeks internship period, I designed and made this footwear to apply the learnings practically, in the industry. I wanted to add something new in the design, so I have added the foam behind the whole upper leather, instead of a separate pattern which was what being usually done in the factory. I also added the punches on the leather, which added up to the uniqueness. After analysing the leather types and colours, the suitable leather was chosen based on the learning in the industry as well as college. Later I also did the finishing through spraying to attain the desirable design.
