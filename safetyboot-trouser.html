Safety Boots & Trousers

Safety boots are known as shielding shoes designed to protect your feet from injuries at the extreme conditions, basically it has a sturdy sole and steel toe cap attached. Trousers are loose fitted and it has panel stitching as design element. This includes lots of raw textures and raw finishes. It is authentic to the core by maintaining and highlighting the wear and tear. It gives the look of Vintage and Antiqueness.

Design Story

During the 6th semester, we learnt about the making of safety boots, and I chose to do this as the final product for final assessment because it needed a pinch of extra efforts comparatively. And when I started thinking about the design, I wanted to add some element to it which matches to the garment as well, so I have added the camouflage printed canvas to the footwear which was also used in the garment. The height of the boots can be reduced by folding it outwards and fastening it with the snap buttons. Regarding the garment, cargo pockets of camouflage printed canvas is added as the element of design to the jacket. For the trouser, it is constructed using panels of patterns put together.
